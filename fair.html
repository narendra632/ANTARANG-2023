Stall Wars - Fair Fest Showdown



Join os on 20th October 2022 at 9:00 AM on the NCER Lawn.


Unleash your business building spirit and transform your innovative ideas into profitable ventures at Stall Wars. This is your chance to create, manage, and showcase your business prowess. We provide everything from tents to tables and a captive audience, while you decide how to make your business thrive. It's your opportunity to demonstrate your management skills and secure significant profits.

Welcome to Stall Wars the heart of "ANTARANG - Innovation Fest", where creativity, entrepreneurship, and innovation come together in a dynamic showcase. Here's what you can look forward to:


Idea Submission

Interested students must fill out the Google Form from below to submit their stall idea. Here are the details to provide in the form:

  • Stall Name: Give your stall a catchy and memorable name.
  • Description: Briefly describe what your stall will offer and what makes it unique.
  • Stall Category: Choose one of the following categories:
    • Food & Beverage
    • Games & Activity
    • Services & Products
    • Fashion & Beauty
    • Others (Your unique business proposal)



Details for the Event


  • Shortlisting:

    After the idea submission phase, we will review and shortlist the stall ideas to maintain a rich variety of categories. Shortlisted participants will be notified.


  • Payment and Setup:

    Once your stall idea is shortlisted, the payment and setup process begins. Each shortlisted team will have to pay a fee of Rs 200 to book their stall in fest, which will include a tent with table,chairs and electricity. There will be a limit of 20 stalls in the fest so submit the idea of your stall asap with clear description of the stall.


  • Here's how you can make your stall successful:

    • Managing Funds: Your stall's success is in your hands. All team members can invest an amount for your business venture and share the profits in the same ratio. You can also seek third-party investments if needed.
    • Negotiate with Local Vendors: Collaborate with local vendors to obtain raw materials or products, and share profits based on revenue generated or articles sold.


  • Your Time to Shine:

    The best-performing stall that captures the attention of the audience and secures the maximum number of votes in the ballot box will be awarded a cash prize of Rs 2,000. Stall owners can persuade the audience to vote for them and claim this exciting reward.


  • Audience and Profits:

    Expect a massive audience, including students from all years and departments of NCER and NMIET. We're also inviting faculty from both colleges, as well as 11th and 12th-grade students from NCER. Feel free to invite your friends and colleagues from other colleges.



  • Remember, a huge crowd means huge profits!
